Facilities

At STI, we provide the learning environment you need to succeed. Our campuses boast modern classroom equipped with the latest technology to enhance your education. Whether you need to practice your skills in specialized labs or unwind in comfortable student lounges, STi offers the facilities to support a well-rounded learning experience.

Computer Laboratory

STI’s computer labs, equipped with cutting-edge tech and industry software, empower CS students to code, build applications, and solve problems collaboratively.

Library

Beyond the code, STI’s library offers a rich resource pool of computer science textbooks to fuel your learning journey, ensuring you have the latest knowledge and research at your fingertips.

Mezzanine

Cracked the code for relaxation? Unwind after a long session in the dedicated mezzanine area, offering comfortable spaces for students to socialize and recharge.
